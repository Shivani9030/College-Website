ABOUT US

Stanley College of Engineering and Technology for Women is an undergraduate and post graduate college in Abids, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. The college was established in 2008. It is affiliated to Osmania University[citation needed] and has been approved by AICTE, New Delhi[2] and the Government of Telangana.
